# Talking Robots

**Date:** January 15, 2008

**Original URL:** https://aboutintelligence.blogspot.com/2008/01/talking-robots.html

---

[**Talking Robots**](https://lis2.epfl.ch/resources/podcast/) is a "podcast featuring interviews with high-profile professionals in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence for an inside view on the science, technology, and business of intelligent robotics".

This podcast is produced at the [Laboratory of Intelligent Systems, EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland](https://lis2.epfl.ch/) and it comes out every two weeks.  
In future posts we will comment some of the episodes. Stay tunned!
