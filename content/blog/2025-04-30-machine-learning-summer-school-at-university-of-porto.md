Machine Learning Summer School coming up!

[Inductiva.AI](https://inductiva.ai), in collaboration with the
[Universidade do Porto](https://www.up.pt) and the support of the AptWind doctoral network, is hosting
a 5-day programme where we will dive into the state-of-the-art Machine Learning techniques and their
impact across different scientific fields. The primary focus will be on Computational Fluid Dynamics
(CFD), but it will also explore broader engineering and scientific computing applications.

- 🗓️ Dates: 19-23 May 2025
- 📍 Location: University of Porto

[Read more details](https://inductiva.ai/events/machine-learning-summer-school)

AptWind is a doctoral network part of the Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions, funded by the European Union.
