I am a Machine Learning engineer and researcher, with a particular interest in Physical AI, AI4Science and
Reinforcement Learning. In the past I co-founded [inductiva.ai](https://inductiva.ai), a
cloud-HPC platform for engineers, and I was one of the initial team members of the
[AlphaFold](https://deepmind.google/science/alphafold/) project at
[Google DeepMind](https://www.deepmind.com). Prior to that, I worked in the Query Formulation
team at [Microsoft Bing](http://www.bing.com) and did research in Machine Learning and Computer
Vision at [Idiap Research Institute](http://www.idiap.ch) and
[École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausane](http://www.epfl.ch), both in Switzerland. I did my
undergraduate studies in Informatics and Computing Engineering at [FEUP](http://www.fe.up.pt), in
Portugal, where I am originally from.
